--- layout: archive title: "Research" permalink: /research/ author_profile: true --- My research is in algebra and algebraic geometry, and focuses on affine group schemes and related structures, such as various nonassociative algebras, homogeneous spaces and manifolds with exceptional holonomy.

I am the main supervisor for one PhD student, Isak Sundelius, since 2024. He is doing his PhD within the Centre for Interdisciplinary Mathematics, and his second supervisor is professor Magdalena Larfors at the Department of Physics and Astronomy.
